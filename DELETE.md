Data Deletion Request for StreetSpot
To delete your StreetSpot account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

In-App: Go to Settings and tap the "Data Deletion Request" button.

Email: Send an email to [Your Email Here] with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

We will process your request and remove all stored personal information within 7 business days.
